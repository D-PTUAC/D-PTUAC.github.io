The Idea

The project aims to develop an intelligent system that can detect and track individuals within crowded environments throughout the day, even if the crowd is wearing similar colors or designs.

Morning Tracking

During the morning hours, the drone possesses the remarkable ability to track and monitor an individual with exceptional precision relying solely on their distinctive facial features

Evening Tracking

The output will be presented through a user-friendly interface enabling real-time identification of individuals of interest
